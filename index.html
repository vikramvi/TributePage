B. R. Ambedkar

a scholar, a social reformer and a leader who dedicated his life to eradicating social inequality in India

Here's a time line of Dr. Ambedkar's life:

  • earlier times - entwined in the coils of caste
  • in the 1890's - the struggle for an education
  • in the 1900's - a guarded gateway, but with a gap
  • in the 1910's - off to Columbia, and on to London
  • in the 1920's - how to organize the downtrodden?
  • in the 1930's - conflict, controversy, and Congress
  • in the 1940's - corridors of power, and the Constitution
  • in the 1950's - embracing the Wheel of the Law
  • since then - becoming a Bodhisattva
